# Plan: Enforce Plan Progress Updates and Archiving in plan_standards.md

**Status:** ✔️ Completed  
**Created:** 2026-03-11  
**Scope:** `plans/plan_standards.md`

---

## Background

Plans have not been kept up-to-date with progress during execution, and completed plans have not been archived. The current `plan_standards.md` has archiving rules and a final-step update rule, but lacks a mandatory rule requiring step-by-step icon updates as work progresses. Two enforcement gaps need to be closed.

---

## Steps

1. ✔️ Add a mandatory **Progress Updates** section to `plan_standards.md` requiring that step icons be updated to `🏗️ In-Progress` when started and `✔️ Completed` when done — immediately after each step, not deferred to a final step. Mark deferral a CRITICAL VIOLATION.
2. ✔️ Strengthen the archiving rule to explicitly state that failing to archive a completed plan before `submit` is a CRITICAL VIOLATION (it currently says "IMMEDIATE ARCHIVING REQUIRED" but does not use CRITICAL VIOLATION language for the pre-submit fallback scan finding).
3. ✔️ Update this plan to reflect completion and archive.
