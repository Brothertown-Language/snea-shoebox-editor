# SPR: Algonquian Grammar for SNEA Shoebox Editor

## Core Concepts
- Polysynthetic structure (Verb as a sentence)
- Animacy (Animate vs Inanimate)
- Obviation (Proximate vs Obviative/4th person)
- Gender: Animate (AN), Inanimate (IN)
- Number: Singular (SG), Plural (PL)

## Verb Classes
- AI: Animate Intransitive (Animate subject, no object)
- II: Inanimate Intransitive (Inanimate subject, no object)
- TA: Transitive Animate (Animate subject acting on Animate object)
- TI: Transitive Inanimate (Animate subject acting on Inanimate object)

## Morphological Slots (Simplified)
- Prefix: Person (n- 1st, k- 2nd, w- 3rd)
- Stem: Core meaning
- Finals: Determine verb class and transitivity
- Suffixes: Obviation, Number, Mode, Direct/Inverse markers
