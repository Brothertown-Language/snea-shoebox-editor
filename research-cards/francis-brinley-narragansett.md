# Research Card: Francis Brinley — Briefe Narrative of the Nanhiganset Countrey (c. 1700)

## 1. Source Description

**Author:** Francis Brinley (c. 1640–1719) — Rhode Island colonist, magistrate, and historian. Compiled a narrative description of the Narragansett country.

**Title:** "Francis Brinley's Briefe Narrative of the Nanhiganset Countrey"

**Date:** c. 1696–1700 (manuscript); published 1900 in *Publications of the Rhode Island Historical Society* 8(2): 69–96.

**Content:** A topographical and ethnographic description of the Narragansett region, including some Narragansett place names and vocabulary.

## 2. Dialect Affiliation

Narragansett (Eastern Algonquian, Y-dialect) — supplements Roger Williams' 1643 data with early 18th-century attestations.

## 3. Estimated Entry Count

Limited — the narrative is primarily topographical/ethnographic rather than a vocabulary list. Contains place names and some cultural terms.

## 4. Orthographic System

Ad-hoc English orthography typical of late 17th-century colonial writing.
